Payment Method

Shop as you go, payment made easy.
The following payment methods are accepted on the popoway.me Online Store:

Zelle®

Accepted currency: USD

If you're a US resident with Chase QuickPay® or Bank of America account enabled, Zelle® is the easiest way to send the payment, virtually and securely.

Request recepient address...

Apple Pay Cash®

Accepted currency: USD

This is an alternative payment network for customers who are US residents, and have an Apple device with Apple Pay Cash enabled.

Request recepient address...

Facebook Payments

Accepted currency: USD

This is an alternative payment network for customers who are US residents, and have a Facebook account with Facebook Payments enabled. Learn more about Facebook Payments here.

Proceed...

PayPal

Accepted currency: USD, CAD, GBP, EUR

This is a payment network for global customers, especially intended for European Economic Area (EEA) or Canadian residents. Opening a PayPal account is not mandatory.

Proceed (PayPal)...

Proceed (Venmo)...

Bitcoin

Accepted currency: BTC

Bitcoin is perfect for customers who perfer to shop in an anonymous approach. Please note that it may take up to 48 hours to process the payment, and such transactions are non-refundable.

Request Bitcoin wallet address...

WeChat Pay

Accepted currency: CNY / RMB

This is the primary payment network for customers who are residents in Mainland China, and have a WeChat account with WeChat Pay enabled.

Learn more...

Alipay

Accepted currency: CNY / RMB

This is an alternative payment network for customers who are residents in Mainland China, and have an Alipay account set up.
